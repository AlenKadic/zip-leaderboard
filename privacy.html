Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 Sep 2025

Zip Community Leaderboard (“we”, “us”, “the service”) lets players record their Zip puzzle results and view community leaderboards.

Summary

  • We collect your name, optional LinkedIn URL, and your scores.
  • For sign-in we collect your email address (via Google or one-time code).
  • Data is stored in Google Sheets / Google Apps Script. We don’t sell your data.
  • Your name, optional LinkedIn link and times are public on the site. Email is never public.
  • You can delete everything using the in-app Delete my data button or by contacting us.

What we collect

  • Account data: name, optional LinkedIn profile URL, email (Google Sign-In or email OTP).
  • Gameplay data: puzzle number, time, derived seconds, and personal best/average.
  • Auth & security: one-time passcodes (OTP, 10-minute expiry) and a session token stored in your browser’s localStorage (30-day expiry). Google may set cookies for Google Sign-In.
  • Technical logs: standard service logs provided by Google Apps Script; we do not intentionally store IPs outside of Google’s infrastructure.

How we use data

  • Provide the service: create your profile, submit and display leaderboards.
  • Authenticate you: Google Sign-In or email one-time code.
  • Operate and secure the service: prevent abuse, debug issues.

Legal bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

  • Contract / legitimate interests: running the leaderboards and your account.
  • Consent: optional LinkedIn link; email OTP delivery; Google Sign-In.

Sharing & processors

We don’t sell your personal data. We use service providers to operate the app:

  • Google LLC – Google Sheets, Apps Script, email (MailApp) and Google Identity.

We may disclose data if required by law or to address abuse, security, or rights protection.

Public information

Your display name, optional LinkedIn URL (as a link icon), and times appear on public leaderboards. Email addresses are never published.

Retention

  • OTP codes: ~10 minutes.
  • Session tokens (localStorage): up to 30 days or until you sign out.
  • Profile & scores: until you delete them.
  • Service logs (within Google): short period consistent with Google’s defaults (typically ≤ 90 days).

Your rights

Depending on your location, you can request access, correction, deletion, portability, or object to processing.

  • Use the in-app Delete my data button to remove your profile and all scores instantly.
  • Or email us at you@example.com and we’ll help.

For CCPA/CPRA, we do not sell or share personal information.

International transfers

Data may be processed on Google’s global infrastructure. Transfers rely on Google’s contractual protections (e.g., SCCs).

Children

The service is intended for users 16+. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we’ll delete it.

Security

We use limited scopes, token verification, and HMAC for OTP/session logic. No unnecessary data is collected. Still, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll change the “Last updated” date and, if material, provide a notice in the app.
